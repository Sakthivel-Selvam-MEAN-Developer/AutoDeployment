# Accounting

## Balance Sheet
Balance sheet contains the following information:
- Assets
  - current liabilities
    - cash
    - accounts receivable
    - inventory
    - prepaid expenses
- Liabilities
  - capital accounts
  - payable
  - accrued liabilities
- Equity

## Income Statement
Income statement contains the following information:
- Revenue
  - Sales
  - Interest
  - Dividends
  - Royalties
  - Rent
  - Other
- Expenses
  - Cost of Goods Sold
  - Operating Expenses
  - Interest
  - Taxes
  - Other
- Net Income
- Earnings per Share
- Dividends per Share
- Dividend Yield
